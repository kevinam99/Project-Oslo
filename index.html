Vision

To create an environment of innovation and fraternalism, assist in effortless dessemination of new ideas and provide a platform for earnest contributions in various fields of engineering.

Mission

To impart and improve technical and non-technical skills & knowledge of members with international standards through a wide range of activities.

Why join IEEE?

  1. IEEE and its members inspire a global community to innovate for a better tomorrow through its more than 423,000 members in over 160 countries, and its highly cited publications, conferences, technology standards, and professional and educational activities.
  2. IEEE is the trusted “voice” for engineering, computing, and technology information around the globe.
  3. The vision of IEEE is to foster Technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity.
  4. IEEE has immense resources which can transform you from a student to a professional and bridge the gap between the academic and industry.
  5. You can stay technically updated and network with peers by being a part of this society.
